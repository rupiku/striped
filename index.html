Tiger

Striped Camouflage

A tiger's stripes help it blend into tall grasses and shadows, making it nearly invisible to prey.

Powerful Swimmers

Unlike most cats, tigers enjoy water and are excellent swimmers, often cooling off in ponds and rivers.

Unique Stripes

No two tigers have the same stripe pattern - their stripes are as unique as human fingerprints.

Strongest Bite

Tigers have the strongest bite force of all big cats, capable of crushing bones with their powerful jaws.

Solitary Hunters

Tigers prefer to hunt alone, using stealth and surprise rather than chasing prey over long distances.
